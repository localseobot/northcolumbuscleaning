## How much does a cleaning cost?

Pricing depends on the size of the space, the type of clean (regular, deep, or move-in/out), and the frequency.

- **Regular residential cleaning:** starts at $100 base, plus square-footage, bedroom, and bathroom adders. Most 3-bedroom homes run $250–$350 one-time.
- **Deep cleaning:** starts at $120 base, with higher per-square-foot and per-room adders. Typically $300–$500.
- **Move-in / move-out cleaning:** same base as deep, usually $300–$500.
- **Recurring discount:** weekly service is 30% off the one-time price, bi-weekly is 25% off, monthly is no discount.

All pricing is flat-rate — never hourly. You get a clear number up front before any work starts.

If you call, Maya (our automated assistant) can give you an exact quote in thirty seconds once you share the square footage, bedrooms, bathrooms, and how often you want service.

## What's the difference between a regular cleaning and a deep cleaning?

A **regular cleaning** is for a home that's already in reasonably clean shape — we handle surface dusting, vacuuming, mopping, bathrooms, kitchens, bed-making, and trash. Usually 2–4 hours depending on size.

A **deep cleaning** is a detail-level top-to-bottom reset. On top of everything in a regular clean, we do baseboards, door frames, ceiling fans, light fixtures, blinds, grout scrubbing, inside the oven, inside the microwave, inside the fridge, cabinet fronts, and switch plates. Usually 4–8 hours.

Most clients get a deep cleaning for their first-ever visit, then switch to regular cleanings on a recurring schedule after that.

## How long does a cleaning take?

Depends on the size of the home and the type of clean. As a rough guide:

- **Regular clean, 1,500–2,000 sqft:** 2–3 hours
- **Regular clean, 3,000+ sqft:** 3–5 hours
- **Deep clean, 1,500–2,000 sqft:** 4–6 hours
- **Deep clean, 3,000+ sqft:** 6–8 hours
- **Move-in / move-out, 1,500 sqft:** 5–7 hours

Crews usually have 2 people, which cuts the wall-clock time roughly in half from a solo cleaner's estimate.

## What's NOT included in a standard cleaning?

Things that aren't in a typical cleaning and usually need a separate scope or quote:

- Exterior window washing
- Interior window washing (on request as an add-on)
- Carpet steam-cleaning or upholstery shampooing
- Laundry (wash-and-fold available as an add-on)
- Washing dishes by hand (loading the dishwasher is fine)
- Pet waste cleanup (inside kennels, litter boxes — we'll tidy around them)
- Biohazard cleanup (blood, mold, etc. — call a specialist)
- Moving heavy furniture
- Climbing to heights above 2-step-ladder reach (ceiling fans and high shelves are OK)
- Cleaning inside a full fridge or oven unless booked as a deep clean

If you need any of these, mention it when booking and we'll either quote it in or let you know it's not something we offer.

## What should I do before the cleaners arrive?

Honestly, not much. If you want to get the most out of the visit:

- **Pick up clutter** from floors and surfaces (clothes, toys, mail, etc.) — we clean better when we can see the surface
- **Secure valuables** — jewelry, cash, important documents
- **Let us know about anything fragile** or delicate we shouldn't move
- **Tell us about pets** — where they'll be, whether we can let them out, any quirks

You don't need to "clean before the cleaners come" — that's our whole job.
