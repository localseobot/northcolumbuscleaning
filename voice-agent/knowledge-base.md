1. **Residential cleaning** — standard house cleans for occupied homes. Kitchens, bathrooms, bedrooms, living spaces, dusting, vacuuming, mopping, trash, bed-making, tidying.
2. **Commercial cleaning** — offices, salons, retail shops, medical suites. Evenings and weekends available so we don't interrupt the workday.
3. **Deep cleaning** — detail-level clean: inside oven/microwave/fridge, baseboards, door frames, blinds, ceiling fans, grout. For first-time visits or seasonal resets.
4. **Recurring service** — weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly. Same crew each visit. Up to 20% off versus one-time pricing.
5. **Move-in / move-out cleaning** — empty-property deep cleans. Inside every cabinet, drawer, appliance, closet.
6. **Short-term rental turnovers** — Airbnb/VRBO. Fast turnovers, linen laundering or swap, restocking, photo-ready staging.

## Common FAQs

**"Do I need to be home?"** — Up to you. Many customers give us a code or lockbox. Our team is trustworthy, insured, and background-checked.

**"What about my pets?"** — No problem, just let us know what to expect (friendly, contained, where they stay). We're not a problem for most animals.

**"Can you clean just one room?"** — Usually yes, though our minimums still apply. The team can confirm based on what you need.

**"How soon can you come?"** — Often within the same week for standard cleans, sometimes sooner for urgent needs. The team confirms availability.

**"Do you offer carpet cleaning / window washing / laundry?"** — Our standard cleans include vacuuming, surface dusting, and bed-making. Deep services like steam-cleaning carpets or interior windows aren't included by default — the team can quote those separately if needed.

**"Do you do construction cleanup?"** — Sometimes, depending on the scope. The team can assess. Post-renovation dust is a deep-clean-plus level of job.

**"I want a quote over the phone"** — We give a clear price once we have the details. You share the size, service, and any special asks; the team comes back within one business day with a flat rate. No hourly surprises.
