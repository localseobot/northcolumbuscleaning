Questions

Frequently asked

Do I need to provide cleaning supplies?

No. We bring everything, including eco-friendly options on request. If you prefer your own products, just let us know.

Are you insured and bonded?

Yes. North Columbus Cleaning Company is fully insured and bonded, and every crew member passes a background check.

How much does a cleaning cost?

It depends on the size of the space, the type of clean, and frequency. Most standard homes fall between $120 and $250. Request a free quote for a clear number.

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

Up to you. Many clients give us access through a code or lockbox.

What if something is missed?

Let us know within 24 hours. We come back and make it right at no extra cost.

How do I reschedule or cancel?

Give us at least 24 hours notice by phone or email. We reschedule at no charge.
