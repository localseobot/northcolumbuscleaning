Questions & answers

Frequently asked questions

Do I need to provide cleaning supplies?

No — we bring everything we need, including eco-friendly options on request. If you prefer us to use specific products from your home, just let us know.

Are you insured and bonded?

Yes. North Columbus Cleaning is fully insured and bonded, and every team member passes a background check before joining.

How much does a cleaning cost?

Price depends on the size of your space, the type of clean, and frequency. Most standard homes fall between $120–$250. Request a free quote and we'll give you a clear, up-front price.

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

It's entirely up to you. Many clients give us access through a code or lockbox. Either way, our team is trustworthy, insured, and respectful of your space.

What if I'm not satisfied with the clean?

We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If anything is missed, let us know within 24 hours and we'll come back to make it right — at no extra cost.

How do I reschedule or cancel?

Just give us at least 24 hours notice by phone or email and we'll reschedule at no charge.
